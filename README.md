# Global Superstore Sales Insights Dashboard

## Overview

The Global Superstore Sales Insights Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, product demand, and regional business performance. The dashboard converts raw sales data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to identify profitable regions, top-selling products, sales trends, and customer purchasing patterns.

The report includes dynamic filtering capabilities that allow users to explore sales performance by region, year, and sales channel, making it easier to uncover business opportunities and monitor key performance indicators.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations operating across multiple regions often struggle to monitor sales performance and identify factors driving revenue and profitability. Decision-makers need a centralized view of business performance to understand:

* Which regions generate the highest profits?
* Which products contribute most to sales?
* How do online and offline channels compare?
* How has sales performance changed over time?
* What is the distribution of order quantities?

This dashboard helps answer these questions through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

---

## Dashboard Features

### Executive KPIs

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Top Selling Product

### Sales Performance Analysis

* Sales Trend Analysis (2010–2017)
* Profit by Region
* Regional Performance Comparison

### Product Analysis

* Quantity Sold by Product
* Online vs Offline Sales Comparison
* Top Performing Product Categories

### Order Analysis

* Order Quantity Distribution
* High, Medium, Low, and Very High Order Segmentation

### Interactive Filtering

Users can filter insights by:

* Region
* Sales Channel
* Year

---

## Key Insights

* Fruits emerged as the top-selling product category.
* Sub-Saharan Africa and Europe generated the highest profits among regions.
* Both online and offline channels contribute significantly to product sales.
* Sales remained relatively stable across years before declining in the latest period.
* Order quantities are distributed fairly evenly across all customer segments.
